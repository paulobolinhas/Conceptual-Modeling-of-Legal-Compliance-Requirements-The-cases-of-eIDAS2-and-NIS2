National Cybersecurity Strategy (NCS)

Strategic objectives and policy measures to achieve and maintain a high cybersecurity level.

1. Strategic Objectives & Priorities
  • Objectives covering sectors in Annexes I & II
  • National cybersecurity goals
2. Governance Framework
  • Roles & responsibilities of stakeholders, authorities, CSIRTs, Single Points of Contact
  • Coordination & cooperation at national & sector levels
3. Risk Assessment & Preparedness
  • Asset identification & risk assessment
  • Preparedness, response & recovery plans
  • Public-private cooperation
4. Policy Measures
  • Supply chain security & vulnerability management
  • Cybersecurity requirements in procurement
  • Education, awareness, training, research & development
  • Information sharing & cyber hygiene
  • Enhancing SME cyber resilience
5. Monitoring & Updates
  • Regular reviews at least every 5 years
  • Assessment based on key performance indicators
  • ENISA support for updates
